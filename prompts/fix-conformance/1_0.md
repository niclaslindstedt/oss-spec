to bring the working repo into compliance with `OSS_SPEC.md` (the §19
checklist).

commands. Prefer minimal, idiomatic content over elaborate boilerplate. For

`ln -s AGENTS.md CLAUDE.md`).

If you need template content you do not know how to write, fetch the
canonical oss-spec reference into a temporary directory with
`oss-spec fetch --into /tmp/oss-spec-ref` and copy/adapt files from there.

Do not commit or push — leave that to the user. When you believe every
listed violation is resolved, stop and print a short summary of what you
changed.
